I play guitar, bass
and harmonica

Music has always been a part of me. Whether it's the deep groove of the bass, the rich melodies of the guitar, or the soulful wail of the harmonica, I love the way each instrument lets me express something different.

In 2014, I formed a band with my colleagues from Cencosud, a multinational company from Latin America. It wasn’t just about playing music—it was about building friendships, sharing creativity, and strengthening our bond beyond the workplace. I always try to have a good connection with my colleagues, whether through music or work. I believe that strong relationships create a better environment, fostering teamwork and collaboration. Jamming together was more than just playing notes—it was a way to communicate, to understand each other, and to enjoy the moment. No pressure, no expectations—just music bringing people together.

I have explored 63 countries

A World traveler with a footprint on every continent and witnessed the Seven Wonders. Adaptable, culturally aware, and skilled at navigating diverse environments and challenges.

I have had the incredible opportunity to explore 63 countries, an experience that has shaped me both personally and professionally. Traveling has enhanced my adaptability, problem-solving skills, and ability to communicate across diverse cultures. Immersing myself in different environments has strengthened my resilience, open-mindedness, and capacity to work with people from various backgrounds. Beyond broadening my worldview, my travels have also reinforced my ability to navigate challenges, think critically, and quickly adjust to new situations—all essential skills in a dynamic work environment. Whether collaborating with international teams or tackling complex projects, I bring a global perspective and a proactive mindset that allow me to thrive in fast-paced and multicultural settings.
